What is

Parkchester

Parkchester is a completely self-contained community within the city. Comfort, convenience and affordability are the hallmarks of every no fee apartment. The reasonably priced apartments are spacious, bright, and airy with many great features including windowed kitchens and bathrooms and hardwood floors. Rentals for one-bedroom apartments start as low as $1290 per month; $1,600 for two-bedroom apartments, and $1,900 for a three-bedroom.

There are over 6,000 Full Serviced Sponsor Apartments at Parkchester managed by Parkchester Preservation Management (PPM). We offer a wide selection of Studio, One, Two and Three Bedroom Apartments.

Why us?

  • Parkchester's 129 landscaped acres contain tall shade trees, lawns, flowerbeds, fountains and playgrounds
  • Complex has more than 100 retail outlets including Macy's, Applebee's, Starbucks, our own medical and dental offices, most major banks
  • Public, private and parochial schools nearby
  • Onsite management and 24-hour security
  • Convenient garages and on-street parking
  • 30 minute commute to Midtown Manhattan by 6 subway station
  • Express bus service to Midtown that stops in the middle of the complex
  • Direct access to 3 major highways
Parkchester

Preservation Management

Parkchester Preservation Management is the exclusive on site rental agent of all sponsor-owned Parkchester apartments and charges no brokerage fees. The management office is conveniently located within the complex at 2000 East Tremont Avenue. Our team at this location provides management services solely for these apartments.

We will treat you fairly with respect and courtesy and you will pay no broker's fee. Your apartment will be freshly painted, spotlessly clean and with all equipment in mint condition. We will process your application promptly and because we have monthly turnover of apartments, you will have a choice of units within your price range and bedroom size preference.
